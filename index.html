About

Believe in yourself and you will be unstoppable...

This came to mind when I decide at 40 years old to quit my job, and return to school. Cosmetology school. Two years later I am still learning. I'm attending classes regularly to keep up with the trends and new styles. I attended Barbering School in March 2016. I will be a certified Cosmetologist with a Barber Cross over in July 2016. I'm very new in this industry, but excited about all the changes this industry has to offer.

I enjoy people. I want to get to know each and everyone of my clients, and hearing about their life adventures. I hope I get the opportunity to be the person you want to share your stories with!

Products

We use All-Nutrient products. These products are 100% Vegan, Gluten-Free, Cruelty-Free, and Sulfate-Free.

These products feature a natural, vegetable-derived base that's infused with active keratin micro-proteins, amino acids, vitamins and minerals, essential fatty acids, and natural oils - all of the building blocks of hair. As it colors, it reconstructs the hair from the inside out, adding strength and moisture. It also uses a very low percent of ammonia. This haircolor is made with the most organic ingredients possible.
